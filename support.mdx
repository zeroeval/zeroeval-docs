## Getting Support

We're here to help you get the most out of ZeroEval. There are two main ways to get support:

### Discord Community

Join our Discord server to:

- Ask questions and get help from the community
- Share feedback and feature requests
- Connect with other ZeroEval users
- Get updates on new features and releases

### Direct Support

  Use the Intercom chat widget inside the ZeroEval app for direct support

## What to Include in Your Support Request

To help us assist you quickly, please include:

- A clear description of the issue or question
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Your team/organization name
- Screenshots or error messages (if relevant)
- The specific feature or page you're having trouble with

## Response Times

- **Discord**: Community-driven support, typically within a few hours during business hours
- **Intercom**: Direct support, typically within 24 hours on business days
